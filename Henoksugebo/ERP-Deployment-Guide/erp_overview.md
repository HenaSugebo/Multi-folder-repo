# README – erp_overview.md

## 📄 File Overview
A high‑level introduction to the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system being deployed, written for both technical staff and business stakeholders.

## 🎯 Purpose
- Align IT and business teams on the **objectives, scope, and architecture** of the ERP solution.
- Provide a quick reference for decision‑makers before diving into technical integration documents.

## 📚 Contents
- **Business Case** – Why the organisation moved from legacy systems to a unified ERP (e.g., inefficiencies in procurement, fragmented finance reporting).
- **Selected Platform** – Brief profile of the ERP software (e.g., Microsoft Dynamics 365, SAP S/4HANA, Odoo). Includes modules:
  - Finance & Accounting
  - Supply Chain & Inventory
  - Human Resources
  - CRM
- **High‑Level Architecture** – Cloud vs. on‑premises decision, tenant topology, and integration points with existing systems (Active Directory, BI tools).
- **Implementation Phases** – Roadmap: discovery, configuration, data migration, UAT, go‑live, hyper‑care.
- **Roles & Responsibilities** – RACI matrix for IT, project management, and business process owners.
- **Success Metrics** – KPIs like order‑to‑cash cycle time reduction, inventory accuracy, month‑end close time.

## 🔗 Related Files
- `integration_steps.md` – technical details on connecting ERP to payroll, CRM, and AD.
- `troubleshooting.md` – post‑go‑live support issues.

## 📌 Tip
Use this document to onboard new project members quickly without exposing them to technical minutiae.
